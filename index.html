MetaMask Wallet - Key Functions

1. Wallet Creation & Import

Create a new wallet or import an existing one using a secret recovery phrase.

2. Secure Key Storage

Safely stores your private keys locally on your device, encrypted with a password.

3. Send & Receive Crypto

Send and receive Ethereum and other ERC-20 tokens easily.

4. DApp Browser Integration

Connect to decentralized applications (DApps) directly from your browser.

5. Network Switching

Switch between Ethereum Mainnet, testnets, and custom networks like Binance Smart Chain.

6. Token Management

Add, hide, or manage various ERC-20 tokens manually or automatically.

7. Transaction History

View your full history of sent and received transactions.
